# Welcome to the Africa Waste Observatory

The Africa Waste Observatory is a comprehensive platform dedicated to advancing research, knowledge, and practice in waste management across the African continent.

## Our Mission

We strive to provide accessible, authoritative information on waste management practices, research findings, and data to support sustainable development goals across Africa.

## What We Do

The Observatory serves as a central hub for:

- **Research Dissemination**: Publishing and sharing academic research on waste management practices, policies, and innovations
- **Data Collection**: Gathering and analyzing waste management data from across the continent
- **Knowledge Sharing**: Providing educational resources and best practices for practitioners and policymakers
- **Network Building**: Connecting researchers, practitioners, and policymakers working on waste management issues

## Why It Matters

Effective waste management is crucial for:

- Public health and environmental protection
- Economic development and job creation
- Climate change mitigation
- Achievement of Sustainable Development Goals

Join us in building a sustainable future for Africa through better waste management practices.
